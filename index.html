DOGGIE -- Haoyue Yao

Introduction

Haoyue Yao (Doggie) is a Chinese new generation rapper. He grew up in Guangdong,Shenzhen. Born in 1998 December. He joined ACE crew in 2020, and went to “ Rap for Youth” in 2020 Fall. Later on he released a few songs made by himself and his crew members.
